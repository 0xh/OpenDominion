Op Center
The Op Center is a great way for you and your realm to demonstrate teamwork. Whenever anyone in your realm performs an information gathering spy or magic operation, the information you gather is posted to your realm's Op Center. Here, everyone has the ability to share scouting information with one another.

Through this page, you can help one another find targets and scout threats to one another. It is important to note that you can only view this page when you are out of protection. Further, you can only view dominions that are within a land range of 40-166% of your size. So if much larger or much smaller dominions are performing operations within your realm, you will not be able to see these in the Op Center. This will also help to filter your list to dominions in your general range, helping you to find information that will be more useful.
