Town Crier
All the news for your realm can be seen here. The town crier gives you news for a 2 day period. You will see only military operations as well as death messages and important messages regarding Wonders of the World. Magical and Spy attacks are not known to the Town Crier, and you will have to inquire in the council as to those types of attacks.
