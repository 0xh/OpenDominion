Construction
Construction is a bit more complicated than it originally looks. Each building comes at a cost of platinum and lumber. Each building falls under one terrain category, which is the only terrain it can be built on. Barren land for each terrain type is shown next to the terrain title. The construction page shows you how many of a certain building you own and how many are currently under construction. At the top of the page it gives the cost of each building, and the number of buildings you can currently afford. In the text at the top of the page, there is a link to destruction, which is a free option to destroy any of your buildings. Captured land due to invasion of other dominions outside of your realm, and at least 75% of your land size, can be built on for 50% of the cost of explored land.
