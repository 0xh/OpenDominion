Diplomacy
Diplomacy deals with your realm's relations with other Realms of the world. There are two types of Diplomacy: War and Peace. Your realm can choose one war relation and one peace relation. Any number of realms can declare relations with you. Peace relations give you a 5% defensive bonus against the realm you declare peace with 24 hours AFTER the peace declaration is made. If you have declared war on a realm, or they on you, both your realms gain +5% attacking strength against each other and +15% land gains from attacks 24 hours AFTER the war declaration is made. If you have mutual war declarations, both realms receive +10% attacking strength against one another and +20% land gains on attacks 24 hours AFTER BOTH war declarations have been made. There is no land gain bonus for a one way war and one way peace set of relations. Realms at war also do more damage with Magic and Espionage than Realms with no relations. Realms that have at least one peace declaration between them receive -10% landgains and 0 prestige on attacks against one another 24 hours AFTER the peace declaration is made. Wonders of the opposing realm may be attacked 24 hours AFTER the first war declaration is declared.
