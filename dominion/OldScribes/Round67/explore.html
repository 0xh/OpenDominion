Exploration
Exploration is done at a cost of platinum and draftees. By allocating these resources, you can gain land in a relatively short time span, without using military force. However, as the size of your Dominion grows, so does the cost. The Exploration page shows you the cost per acre of exploration, as well as the maximum acreage you can explore for.
