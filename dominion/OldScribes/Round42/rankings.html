Rankings
The rankings screen offers you a host of options. You can see rankings based on land total or networth total for either single Dominions or their realms. You can also look at the individual rankings by race or alignment, for an added look at how each of the races are doing. Other rankings can be viewed for prowess in attacking, exploring, magic and espionage. This is the place you can also see the build status of the Wonders of the World to check to see who owns what.
