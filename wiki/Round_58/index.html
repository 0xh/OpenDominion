General Information

Round 58 saw the introduction of the castle improvement Harbor, previously known as Irrigation.

Roundwin story

Well here I am once more, winning my 2nd round but of the last 4.

Having kept no records of my hits or what went on pretty much, I'll just sum my round up as briefly as I possibly can. ;p

I decided to go with the same strat as I did in round 52. Hit with all hobbo OP out of the gate and explore to ~1k in the hours after OOP using the hobbos for DP. I went with that idea mostly because I don't like taking the risk of being hit 12hrs out despite leaving >9k DP. Unlike round 52 I was prepared for the explored/incoming acres cap and better defended this time. My packies were Ed (fast converting gobbo), spooks (converting troll), Arthryn (liz), chris ("fast" converting icer) and Zedijar (allround attacking troll).

Sim was 650a with 1250 hobbos and 1.1k shamans OOP, more shamans incoming the hours after. OOP went really bad considering 7k OP got me less than 150a and I ended up spending more plat on exploration than I had planned. Still ended up on just above 1k as anticipated and sat there training up hobbos/shamans to finally hit again on day6 I think. I decided to go with early DMs and had 150 already at that point, skipping out on the usual 50-ish schools, which I shouldn't have after all. :p The other attackers were about the same size as me when I started to hit again, but none of them had any DMs IIRC.

I kept hitting as much as possible every 12hrs until I reached around 4.5k hobbos at which point I started to train WR for OP. The competition was narrowed down to #29 (evil fast attackers), #11 gobbo, #2 halfers, #24 halfers and #12 FW by that time. It was around week 3 I believe when #2 (who had fallen behind a bit) suicided #11 gobbo, who in return released/razed and farmed out. Conveniently enough #2 suicider got hit within 40 seconds by #24, I'm still convinced some kinda foul play was involved there, but after all I don't care much now anymore. #29 never really turned out to be big threats in the long run so it was down to Rio(#12), Merf+Rommel(#24) and me+Ed, who had converted early with no hobbos and better imps than me, but he didn't really manage to grow at the same pace.

Things kept going on normally, everyone growing while trying to stay safe from each other until we ran out of targets (probably somewhere between day30 and 35, not sure again because of no records) and started to train 4:3 OP. #24 was a bit larger than me but with better imps my economy was at least as good as theirs. When they finally sent out they tried to cover for each other, but since I was deadset on not letting them win and we could double Merf (biggest dom at the time) we went for it. I kinda feel bad for talking Ed into suiciding at that point, especially since Merf didn't get hit after our hits on him but fed by #33 late converting gobbo who decided to hit Ed (clever move -_-). Rommel came out of this as largest dom with lots of barren though and Merf was severly hurt at that point, all he would do until his dom finally died was train 4:3 to sui me when I'd give him the chance.

Sitting continued until Rommel decided to send out hoping Merfs sui op would keep me from hitting him. I checked whether I could make a safe hit anywhere but found none, so I hit Rommel (~120% hit IIRC) and took the suicide from Merf who in turn lost 3k or so acres and died off completely (accompanied by some funny ingame messages, care to explain again how I "made your realm win" please? :D). What followed were a few back and forth hits, due to #24 having Portals. We had taken Ruby earlier and it turned out to be a very good choice. We exchanged hits 3 times, each of which I took more acres and less overall casualties than Rommel (a bit more offensive but WAY fewer defensive casualties thanks to Ruby+Shaman reduction). I ended up on 7k acres with 2k barren, around 600a bigger than Rommel who had a lot less acres to build though. He made one more hit to put him about 100a behind, while I upped GTs from 5% to 20% over the next days until I finally had all landtypes but caverns built.

I waited until day48 when someone finally entered my 75% range with little enough dp to allow me to leave 550k dp thus being safe from suis and wrap the round up. Remaining hits were below EG range hits to finish a bit larger.

Rio, slz, bure - Good rounds, well played.

#24 - You guys are ridiculous, I won't even go into the farm-or-not-farm issue but the way you whine and post info on the boards and then go ballistic when someone does the same thing to you is a joke. Merf showed his true face with his ingame messages (nazi insults are classy) and it makes me feel even better about this win; taking you guys down 1.5vs2 and walking away with it when all I really wanted was to not let you win this. MSL, you can flame and talk down my round all you want I played it safe after I got into a winning spot, so Ed's effort wouldn't go to waste; you on the other hand were a non-factor all round even with all the protection you had. Rommel, well played despite that one dubious hit.

Anyway, this round is done, I'm happy with the way it worked out for me and I'll be back to tie it with Lorian soon!
