Landtypes

There are seven different landtypes and buildings are tied to a specific landtype, except for Homes, whose landtype depend on the race.

Plain Mountain Hill Swamp Forest Cavern Water
Buildings: Alchemy, Farm, Smithy, Masonry Ore Mine, Gryphon Nest Factory, Guard Tower, Shrine, Barracks Tower, Wizard Guild, Temple Lumberyard, Forest Haven Diamond Mine School Dock
Races: Human, Nomad, Troll Dwarf, Gnome, Icekin Halfling, Goblin, Kobold The Nox, Undead, Spirit Orc, Sylvan, Wood Elf Dark Elf, Firewalker, Lycanthrope Lizardfolk, Merfolk
