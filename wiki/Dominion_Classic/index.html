Dominion Classic

Dominion Classic, colloquially referred to as Dominion or Classic (see Name issue, is an online text-based strategy game with around 1200 active players.

Description

The front page of the game says:

You have reached the online game Dominion. What is Dominion? It's a TOTALLY FREE online fantasy war game. You take control of a plot of land in a realm consisting of a group of other players. With your dominion and allied players, you combat the forces of good or evil using military, magic, or espionage. The goal? To finish at the top of the heap. To make your dominion and your realm the most powerful in the land, and to crush your enemies.

Gameplay

Dominion is divided in rounds which last 50 days each. Players may join at any time during these 50 days but it is most common to sign up during what is called registration. No matter when the player joins the game, the first 72hrs will be under protection. If a player signs up with less than 72hrs left of the game, the player will not experience OOP.

Once signed up and verified (email verification is required), the player is given a dominion (which can be likened to a nation) which in turn is inside a realm. All dominions start at the size of 250 acres and with a minute army. When registering, the player is asked to pick a Dominion name, ruler name and race.

Dominion can be played in many ways, including:

  • Attacking
  • Converting
  • Exploring

System requirements

A web browser that can run Javascript. So if you are using your computer, any web browser made after 1942 will suffice. If you are logging in from a phone, most new phones' default browser comes with Javascript, but for ease, you can download Opera Mini to make logins from your phone. iPhones can use the Safari application for logging in as well.

Name issue

The term Dominion may in a strict sense apply to both Dominion Classic and Dominion Liege Lord. This is in reality not the case, since players using the word "Dominion" do in fact mean Dominion Classic. In a context where both games are discussed, players may refer to Dominion Classic by using the term Classic. Liege Lord is commonly called LL.
