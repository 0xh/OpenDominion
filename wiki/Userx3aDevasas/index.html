One of the "veteran" players at least in the time spent playing dom (Started back somewhere in round 6 and is has been playing ever since (albeit with several breaks)). Never did really good, because he tends to get bored by mid-round and then becomes semi inactive resulting in 4-5k land finishes at best. Has a preference for all-round attacking fast races, most notably lizardfolk for evil and dwarf for good. Top finish with a dwarf in round 29: Screaming Orgasm Dwarf 7,244 1,390,962.

When playing can be often seen in the dominion mIRC chatroom.

Also known as dev.
