General Information

Roundwin Story

Okies. I know its a bit late, but here is my/our round.

I came oop on 678 acres with a little over 4k op and 4500 dp, mostly warriors and miners.

I logged in 1 hour late for my warrior training, so oop was wave 7 for me.

Some people think I set up a farm for oop, but I truly didnt though it may have looked that way. Of course im aware I hit someones farm, but thats how the game is.

Anyway, oop looked like this: 6:02:05 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 56 land from Hiyu (# 26). 6:01:59 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 71 land from Hiyu (# 26).

After my daily I was in a decent sit and just kept hitting more or less 75-80% for the next 2 weeks. I was mostly worrying about good top OP unfortunately, thanks to pepsii and the suiciders in #32. We werent too worried about them in the long run though, since pepsii was running a lot of specs and #32 was just there to suicide it seemed. During that time i didnt have more than 5k miners, just enough to take care of the ore for the warriors.

day 5: 2:23:07 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 56 land from Audio Blood (# 3). 2:17:57 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 58 land from Onderan (# 10).

day 6: 2:41:24 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 67 land from Hellstorm (# 67). 5:56:57 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 69 land from Full Metal Alchemists (# 69).

day 7: 9:41:51 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 83 land from Tick Tack Tuck Tock Teck (# 27). 9:08:03 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 88 land from J. Alfred Prufrock (# 45).

day 8: 10:58:56 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 110 land from The Pterodactyl (# 49).

day 9: 10:59:12 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 99 land from Kabok Qi Mokat (# 13).

day 10: 1:53:03 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 114 land from Grab Thy Hand and Forfeit (# 45).

Around here we decided to get a wonder, and FoY was the easy choice at the time. I think we got it at less than 400k hps and had it for ages :)

day 14: 11:34:42 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 168 land from Dragon Warrior (# 22).

day 15: 1:42:33 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 142 land from More Bark Than Bite (# 45).

By this time merf (nights plutonian shore #53) was getting nasty, and had top op or close to it, which was very good for me. But #32 merf was growing fast and had potential to get nasty because the hit he had just made. A good race with top op would be disasterous for me and sami (fury), so i decided it was time to send some clerics at him. Merf was informed about this and said he was up for hitting him as well.

Merf hit him first and then i followed;

day 16: 5:34:56 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 241 land from Never Say Never Again (# 32).

By doing this I was well aware that the dwarf in #4 was close to or able to suicide me. Depsite he had sent me several messages in-game that he would suicide me because of my "oop farming" I figured it was worth the risk in order to stop #32 merf.

The obvious happened;

day 17: 1:36:55 AM Sinistral of Destruction (# 4) invaded Desolation (# 54) and captured 230 land.

Because of the reduced defensive losses and clerics of course it didnt hurt me that much since my economy and dp at this stage was pretty good, thus i was able to keep hitting.

I think it was around this time that the 2 halfers in #10 decided to war us and try to sink me, they didnt do a very good job at it, so I had plenty of time to ad like 365 docks (~8%) iirc. plus i had 1 spa at that time so they failed a lot. So I kept hitting;

day 17: 2:24:47 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 177 land from BigFluffyBerserkerDonkey (# 66).

day 19: 12:58:59 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 219 land from Blades of Steel (# 14).

day 20: 2:59:07 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 217 land from Riders of Rohan (# 9).

day 24: 1:35:20 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 287 land from Never Say Never Again (# 32). 6:19:04 PM Fellow dominion Desolation (# 54) fended off an attack from Never Say Never Again (# 32).

day 25: 1:49:50 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 209 land from Dtechnolife Jack SpaRrow (# 48).

day 28: 2:59:19 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 273 land from Remember Yesterday (# 52).

day 30: 1:59:37 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 293 land from Gimme all your coffee (# 10).

day 31: 5:57:40 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 274 land from Timelessly Worthwhile (# 21).

day 34: 2:58:12 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 374 land from Yellow Knife (# 7).

day 38: 11:56:15 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 442 land from Warkwarkwark (# 58).

day 41: 2:59:34 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 507 land from LegendaryLizardfolk86 (# 57).

I'm not exactly sure when we (not all in the pack though) decided to NAP merf (#53) and play domionion police and take care of the many rounds cheating #21 troll (traex) but Ross did a great job and fixed that for us eventually. We then cancelled the NAP with merf and that made him pretty irritated it seems, so he warred us and posted some msn logs on the board. We decided to war him back though he had nasty op for me to hit him all in. I then trained for that;

day 45: 1:58:58 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 661 land from Nights Plutonian Shore! (# 53).

Admittedly I lauged my arse off after that hit. It was all in with ares on :P

I was then out af all attackers range but the ones in my realm, so i started suiciding explorers;

day 45: 11:01:13 PM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 495 land from Timmys Place (# 23).

day 47: 10:26:26 AM Victorious on the battlefield, Desolation (# 54) conquered 524 land from Green leaves of Summer (# 56).

My plan was to hit #56 sylv the last day, but other troll in #21 had gotten into my range, and I dont even think I had the op to do it actually.

Anyway, the last 4 days of the round I spent under the sun in spain so didnt pay much attention to what happened.

Somewhere in there we decided to mess with bure, mostly because of his suiciding realmies. So we warred #4 and quaked him (love me 2 times baby). They knocked down fountain and we took it back. When #4 at a later point wars #53 to take lair, we war #4 and take lair. They slowly take lair back while we slowly hit obelisk in #53.


----------


Anyway great round and much fun. And its sad dominion had to go down to 1500 players before i got my chance to win :P

I want to thank packmates (celt, spooks, rommel, fingal and ed) for doing such a great job helping me to win. This was a team effort!

And many thanks to the other pack (erix, lars, spoox, volv and mannowrath)


-emesel
