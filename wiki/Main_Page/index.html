Featured Article — Sparticus' Guide to Simming

"Do not underestimate the importance of protection. In its infancy your dominion is weak, undeveloped, and in a state of disarray. You must nurture her so she can be big and strong when she comes out of the womb to face the harsh and cruel world of Kamikazia. The choices you make during this stage of the game will have lasting consequences, and can be the difference between a solid finish and a slow painful round.

This guide is not the be-all and end-all of simming. Perfecting sims requires tweaking, which will not be the focus of this guide. I will provide a foundation for you to build upon. I'm not going to give you a rigid set of rules to abide by so you can mindlessly replicate cookie cutter sims. I'm going to lay out basic strategies and explain the reasoning behind them. If you fancy yourself an average or below average simmer, then I can pretty much guarantee that you will notice immediate improvement in your sims upon completion of this guide."
