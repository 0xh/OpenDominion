General Information

The first win for firewalker and the third exploring win ever, however it was the first of which was done without the support of a 2nd packed explorer running the same strat.

Roundwin story

Wow! I actually did it, became the first explorer to 9k in recorded history (since rd18) while holding off the best player of all time, preventing him from that elusive record setting third victory. A most satisfying round if there ever was one.

Pre-round: I asked Erix if he had room in his pack and I joined with Nati, Violetta, Ronddraai and Gallagher. When I learned that Nati was in the pack I was excited because she’s the biggest ‘good luck’ charm in the game having pack wins in the last two base rounds. Great job girl, you helped make it three in a row, definitely the best all around packie in the game!

My strat was simple, improve on my already nasty fw sim but make sure I had enough peasants to use all my plat. Rd52 I teched which saved me 20m plat but slowed me down too much so I decided to go pure speed. On a side note, in this story I reference my rd50 pace and mav’s pace fairly often. The finishes those rounds were 8151, my personal best, and 8890, the all time record, respectively.

OOP: I came OOP at 836 acres and 63k income, 8th overall. The initial threat was #31 so we peaced them a few hours in.

Ok here’s the part everyone has been waiting for…

Day 34: In an epic failure, Slater hits too close to the hour change which allowed Burnt to suicide in the same tick. The best part for me about this was that the only guys with net land gains were 2nd tier attackers. Plus the #2 explorer, another fw, also gets hit so my exploration lead jumped to 1500 acres.

Day 42-43: A flurry of attacks cut my lead from 1045 to 180, but since I had so much dp, I kept exploring a steady 8 acres/hr. Two unsung moments that I couldn’t have won without can be attributed to vapro and xEnvious. Vapro single-handedly sunk Lorian, who chose to train 10k spies instead of building docks. Had those 10k units been specs instead, he would have won the round. xEnvious’s realm #34, took down both Lair from #33 and OoP from #39 in the same day! Lorian gets ToD which allowed him to hit out of vapro’s range.

Day 45: The peak of my economy: 492k income, 82k peasants. If you count gem conversion, which I did almost every hour the final 15 days, I was at 509k income. Some gems went to food since I was swarmed 24/7 the final week.

Day 46: I think it was this point when I realized that I was the favorite to win the round, I trained 6k specs to make sure I could defend 4:3+spec pumps. Meanwhile the top ops were keeping each other home and running on 30k pez. Lorian was in the best position with 40k lol

Day 48: At this point I was already fantasizing about the win after achieving a 1200 acre lead and I let Erix war #40 so that he and Nati could have fun sinking an exposed human. Burnt gets Lair and both he and Lorian make pretty decent grabs which quickly cut that down. I cursed myself for being so stupid when it became clear that Lorian would need to be sunk for me to win. Robbery had been getting so insane and I had outgrown my towers lately that I had no choice but to make all 24 logins each day within a few seconds. This completely killed me physically and I’ve been getting run down and sick as a result. I didn’t bounce many but here’s a few of the guys

Day 49: Lorian left himself with a 40h window to make 3 hits. At this point I figured I’d finish 9k so I didn’t see how I could win the round. Lorian’s 2nd grab wasn’t as big as I thought so I was renewed with anticipation. At this point, I also discovered that his packie, Takao, I play for fun! seRiously (#41) was 12k away from breaking me. I never would have seen it coming.

Day 50: Its not too often that a round comes down to the final day. I stayed up all night taking a few naps running through the scenarios. Lorian had pumped 33k worth of specs but he would now need to bf into the top 25 so it would be a close finish. Lorian needed to make a 177 acre grab to beat me and when I saw that he could only muster 139 I was able to breathe the biggest sigh of relief. Its been 21 rounds since we’ve had such a close finish.

Closing Comments:
While I can attribute the win to luck, the fact is luck was only a part. Rd50 I explored to 8k with a double runaway and finished third. If this had been a more typical op round I probably would have finished around 8300 and in 2nd or 3rd place. The fact is, training military is such a small portion of the budget it isn’t going to sway an explorer any more than 1k either way.
This round it was I suppose its typical for a winner to say something like ‘This was gonna be my last round win or lose’ so I’ll say something along those lines. I’m retiring from exploring. After 4 exploring titles, 5 top 10s, two top 3s and now a victory and the all time record, its time to prove myself as an attacker.
