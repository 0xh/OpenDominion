Macros writes about Realm #11

Wacky round.

When we put our pack together we were pretty sure that we HAD to win the round or it'd be a failure, rommel, emesel, mav, morty, virchue and myself simply constituted awesomeness (in our own minds at least).

Planning started, and so did much, much arguing, where one side of the table argued strongly for evil, and the other side argued strongly for good (due to kobolds being massively overpowered). This went on for about a full week untill finally we made the decision to go evil after days of obsessive simming.

Rommel and emesel would go with a goblin with heavy dmines and schools and offensive specs out of the gates, I designed a dark elf strat which Mav and me would be running and Morty and Virchue would be running liz for sui power and support.

After a productive prot we all kept up nicely, but in a fairly anonymous way, not pressuring the various top attackers around but getting acres left and right, with a bit of help from Talium who was playing a random, exploring teching dark elf with mad spies.

Rommel got hit around 2000 acres and basically quit, Emesel followed. I can't explain how much it sucked having them quit after one fairly minor setback, but quit they did.

In the meantime, both Mav and myself were doing nicely, but mav got hit and was forced to keep training swordies longer then he was planning while I went on to SW's, ready to start phasing our swordies.

Then sht happened; realm 10 decided that they wanted to have the fountain, and because "they were bored" decided to sink us both despite the fact that we were NO threat to anyone.

This forced us into plan B; we both had to release all of our swordies and do a sort of mini convert, switching to all spirit warriors.

By the time we were done we had both gotten magical weaponry, so despite falling back to 15th and 25th we felt we were still going to have a good shot at winning the thing, if only the top would slow down a bit.

In the meantime, morty seemingly managed to pounce on every suicider in sight and was getting pretty big, this served us well later on.

After awhile stuff started slowing down pretty bad, mostly due to Burnt (Sundown #51) being a complete maniac and everyone being deathly afraid of getting semi'd or totally sui'd by him.

Burnt proceeded to prod, annoy, insult and threaten me for a few days, and accused us of having no balls. At some point we decided we had taken enough abuse from him, and when he sent out, Morty popped a legendary sui on him, and I followed, effectively ending burnt's round.

This we considered was the true tipping point, only the 2 lycs were ahead of us, and we knew they'd never be able to compete vs 8 point adepts and 7 point spirit warriors.

So we kept going and then everything locked down, mav started bottomfeeding while I kept pumping more and more OP, allowing for smaller converters (mostly nox) to catch up the the all round attackers.

I managed to hit asanka (47 gob) twice before the end of the round and got over 10K, and so did Mav. Morty and Virchue both ran into some bad luck at various stages during the round and didn't manage to get high personal ranks, but were instrumental in the pack victory, Talium finished third in the pack and basically had an epic round after starting so poorly.

Despite the fact that people tossed all kinds of veiled insults our way for the way we played it fact remains that a dual 10K finish hasn't been done since round 16; so I like to believe our round WAS special.

Thanks to all the guys that played with us, special mention goes to Herc (black magic) and Talium, both of them did everything to help despite having no obligation to do so, having gone random.

I hope this story sucked less then the one I put up in round 45 ;)

Mac
