Roundwin Story

Well here is my review of round 21 (i try to not bore you too much with too many early round details and if you think it's still too long then don't read it^^): I chose to play Spirit this round since i expected few Gobbos and alot humans and because my pack prefered to go evil. Else the race of my choice would have been Human since i think they are stronger then Spirits (also from the evil races Gobbo is better then Spirit imo but i played them 2 rounds ago and had no motivation to play them again). I also got a very cool pack together (Kenntoft, Kuki, Scrappy, Percy and Samantha).

I planned to play a runaway Spirit with 50% smithies and high factories to grow quickly early. Then at about 3k acres i wanted to get rid of my smithies and replace them with Dmines to play a masonry/factory strategie lateround. Most of it i managed like i planned...i just did the razing of my Dmines not right when i wanted but it was better in the situation back then (i write beneath why).

For the start of the round i got Wave 6 which was fine for me since i prefer that wave above all others. Thx to the low food consumption of Spirits i was able to run a strat without farms during the protection time. Also i was able to find a strat that run completly without specs unlike all other Spuds who came OOP with Banshees as defense.

The conversion and land disadvantage brought me into a bad position compared to the other spirits on top. This gave me the advantage for having huge imps lateround but also put me into a bad position that moment: everyone else had smithies and i had a bad income too so i fell behind in training troops (still don't know why i was able to keep up after razing my smithies).
