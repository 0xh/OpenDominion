Rio's Story

We decided that going Good would be a nice choice this round and hoped we'd be the top Good pack so that we could take ToB without too much trouble when it came out. Looking at races, Bell had decided on merf, ladeda and fingal both wanted dorf. I was torn between sylvan, FW and halfer. I wanted to show that sylvan were still playable but when Ross gave halfer 40% reduced cas making them super-charged FWs, the choice was made for me. ladeda had made a pretty decent sim for me that I tweaked with different buildings and came up with an impressive OP 12 hours out. By reading the boards and suggested DPs, I would be able to hit a LOT of people 12 hours after wave 6 came OOP. I gave the sim to Margarita so she picked halfer too. I do kinda feel bad that we convinced Dreki to go sylvan since he really wanted human instead but we were after speed and ops help with wiz races (especially DEs) so we pretty much forced him into it (sorry bud).

After going with a slow race last round, and wussing out from do-or-die strats in the past, I decided this would be the one where I'd go crazy specs and mods early. The plan was for me to go out as fast as possible and to take out as many Good attackers as I could and then have fingal and ladeda going fast as well tucked in right behind me for when my inefficiency started to hurt, so that they could then take over and cover me. I also kinda figured I'd get caught out at some point and then I'd switch to ops bitz for the others which is another reason as to why I was thinking of halfer in the first place.
