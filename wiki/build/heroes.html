Heroes

From The Dominion Encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Heroes (at some points in the game called (great) military leaders; a term never used elsewhere) used to be a premium-only feature until round 40 when they were made available to everyone. At that point, all they did was go up in level for every successful invasion and on every invasion on targets greater than 75% your size. Heroes emerge with a certain probability on invasions of dominions greater than 75% your size. They are given a random name based on a set of race-specific first and last names. Because of this, one dominion may have several heroes with the same name although this is relatively rare. To combat confusion, heroes are now listed with their class and level on the Invade page.

After heroes had been available to everyone for one round round, Ross introduced a class-based hero system (which was first tested/used in Liege Lord before being added to Dominion Classic). Heroes start at level 0 with no bonus and may go up 1 level for every successful invasion on a target that is 75% or greater of your size that he is sent out on (maximum is level 10). The bonus from a hero affects you even if the hero is returning home from an attack. Heroes return home with the last units, meaning that if a hero is sent out with 9hr-units and some regular units, the hero will return with the regular units.

New Changes

In Round 68, Ross has made several tweaks to the hero system. Heroes will now start out with 8 heroes at level 0 of each class and they no longer die. They also have lower bonuses as compared to their predecessors, 60% of previous values, to compensate for their immortality.

In Round 71, Heroes level up based on hero experience gain depending on the number of acres of land conquered (not generated). Heroes also advance one level at a time regardless of XP (land) gain. Eg. If you have a hero with 0 experience that invades someone for 4001 land, they do not jump from level 0 to level 10, they only advance to level 1.

Before Round 68

The following equations and list of bonuses (below) are no longer applicable and are kept solely for historical references

Heroes' Bonuses: 

base * e^(level/3.3)

with e^ being the exponential function. You can use Google to calculate this. In this example the base is 0.65 (a Farmer) on level 4, which gives a bonus of +2.18%, or you can see the list below.

On every invasion, a hero risks death. The base probability for death is 15% on a successful invasion, and 30% on a failed invasion. Similarly to the bonus formula, the risk of death increases with every level. For successful invasions, the probability of death is: 

15+2*level

whereas for failed invasions it is: 

30+2*level
