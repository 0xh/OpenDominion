Spirit/Undead are fast/medium speed races that are more or less the same thing. The major difference is that Spirit belongs to the Good alignment and Undead to the Evil alignment. These two races always seem to have the most people playing them round after round, probably because SPUD is one of the most unique races in dominion. All of their troops are immortal (in most circumstances) and when they attack they can convert the enemy's casualties into more of their own units. The basic idea of attacking with SPUD is to hit in the proper range as often as possible using elite OP in order to generate free elite DP units for your dominion. Because they are a fast/medium speed race, it is recommended to have started building Guard Towers by mid-round.

Tech rounds

During the Tech Ruleset, Spirit is removed and only Undead is playable. In this ruleset, the Vampires are given a new look. They can also convert Progeny from targets as small as 50% of your size, which allows you to continue gaining conversions if you do not have enough OP to hit larger targets. Additionally, a major addition for Vampires was using techs to gain different abilities. Obtaining the Magical Weaponry tech, the Vampires gain 1 offense point for every 12.5% Wizard Guilds to a maximum of +2 OP.

General information

Pros

  • Undying units
  • Converts enemy casualties on attack (this is the best unit ability that exists within Dominion)
  • No ore cost, no boats needed, very few farms needed - lots of extra room for homes and mods
  • Population bonus
  • Great turtle
  • Can be a very fun race

Cons

  • Very inefficient
  • Very expensive
  • Units can be killed by HuNo, or in attacks that fail by more than 85%
  • Hard to hit often while keeping enough DP home
  • Requires a lot of time for target searching in order to keep hitting

Good for

  • Experienced attackers with lots of time
  • Players trying for "Most Victorious Dominion"
