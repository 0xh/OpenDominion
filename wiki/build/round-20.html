Roundwin Story

I packed with Morphling, PsStT, Wart, Chris and Matt (PsStT's brother). I was hoping to play spud, since i thought they would be very strong with the huge food bonus they got. 'Too bad', i got Goblin, wave 5.

Since i was to lazy to make a oop sim, morph made me a great one. I came oop with 4K defense, and something like 9.200 offense (that adds in 150*6 our icekin put in realmimps oop). Oop, i didnt have a premium (morph did me some searches though), so i had a hard time finding targets, and i did quite bad considering my offense, grabbed about 300 acres if i remember correctly.

A couple hours later i got grabbed by one of the Goblins in 190. I was quite suprized, since i had one of the top networths and a decent defense. Usually thoose dominions dont get grabbed oop. I saw in my towncrier the other strong people got hit aswell by 190, and the other top NW gobbo got a hit aswell. Later i heared thoose were Rambo's multies, smacking the competition. The 190 gobbos were the first guys to land on my 'to hit' list.

9 hours after i didnt dare to send all (to defend again 190) and that resulted in 930 acres or so 21 hours oop. On thoose acres i think i got something like:
450 smithies
147 homes
83 farms
35 ore mines
70 alchs {leftovers from oop}
40 factories {leftovers from oop}
40 lumber yards
65 towers
30 docks

I trained up a save defense and offense and started grabbing. Since there were many fast races i wasnt straight on top early. I hang around somewhere in the top 20. Training defense to defend top op, and increasing my offense steadily.

around 2000 acres i had something like:
450 smithies
400 homes
180 farms
200 ore mines
70 alchs {leftovers from oop}
40 factories {leftovers from oop}
90 lumber yards
135 towers
60 docks
375 diamond mines

The build was aimed at a big income. I kept enough homes to fill all the jobs i had. As a realm, we also decided to go for realmimps, and we would all run 10% ore mines from 2K acres and onwards. Simply build farms/towers etc etc. On the rest of the acres i didnt used for thoose things i build diamond mines.

around 2K, i had a big offense, and after doing another grab and training up, i had savely sendable top op. This was also the point we took the Temple of the Damned. I was pretty scary now. While my troops were returning i spied on some people in the top, and i noticed some big gobbo (Malaysia... Truly asia) with troops out, and i could quite easily hit him. He checked me back, and saw i could hit him. I left to watch some soccer, since my troops werent back anyway, and decided to hit that gobbo in the commercial break. When i came back i was fairly suprized, realm 27 declared war on me. That realm contained a couple small HaLL halfers. Since i didnt have any guard because i hit the wonder a couple hours earlier, they were in my range. They demo'ed me to 0%, so i coudnt make a hit. I was quite sad, because i realized that by the time i was in rg, they could grow into that range, and by the time i was in eg, they could be there too, so i considered my round as over. One of my realmies helped me out bigtime here: Chris. He hit thoose hobbbos out of my rg rang, so i could starting get morale back. Thanx a lot for that chris. Right before i joined royal guard, they gave me a good sinking though. It was easy to figure Malaysia.. Truly asia was responsible for the demo/sinking session, so i added him to my to hit list.

Since i ran decent docks, i had enough boats to almost full send by the time i had my morale back. (about 3 days after getting the TOD). Noone big was out now though, so i had to settle with some smaller goblin. when my troops returned 9 hours later, i attacked some top 10 firewalker (thanks Wart for the draftee killing here), and then i hit one of the 190 goblins. This would be one of the many retals done this round. I was top dominion when all of thoose acres came in. I build homes for full employment, and a load of diamond mines. Again started training to a big op, and now i hit the other goblin in 190 twice.

my imps got decent with the decent gem production, and since i stopped building dm, i had space available. This space got filled with Masonries.

around 5K, rambo (Malaysia.. Truly asia) and me were somewhat ahead of the other people, both land and military wise. We both had the op to hit eachother if we would send, so we used some sort of trick: sent just seconds before the hourchange. This means, that if one decides to hit the other, the other can retal, and visa versa. This resulted in us both staying away from eachother.

Then rambo did something interesting: he send 11 minutes AFTER a hourchange. i noticed that about 10 minutes later. He left about 2K more defense than i had offense though. I just had to nail him. after all he did this round i coudnt let him go, while i was this close. I checked how i could up my offense and lower his defense. The options were my realmimps, my forges for increasing my op, and kill dratees and raze rambo to lower his dp. Luckily i had a realmie (Wart) that saved about 1 million ore for when he would be done converting. He decided to help me out and invested in it in my realmimps. Also, he and me killed some of rambo's draftees. Still, i was slightly short, so i had to see if i could get him razed. Razing shoudnt happen of course, but this guy is such a lamer, so i didnt mind using it on him. Thing is: it's kinda hard to find someone willing to raze, since they gain nothing from it and you will get a nice grab. t00th hated rambo badly though, so he razed 210 of his smamans to dead. i now had enough op (by 50 points) to hit rambo. This resulted in 500 acres for me, and my main competer loosing 500. he couldnt get me back, so he got t00th demo'ed to 0% , razed a couple of times and Lightning Bold badly. Then he grabbed him all-in twice. Sorry for that t00th, if i knew that would happen, i woudn't have asked. Thanks a lot for the help here.

Because rambo was grabbing to regain acres, his op dropped, and i grabbed four days in a row at that point i think, so i got quite big fast.

A couple days later i hit romothecus, but then rambo did something i didnt think of. He had his alliance to hit the Lair of the Dragon, and then his realmie did the final hit. So now rambo had suddenly 10% more op, and he hit me for 818 acres. he lost his wonder a couple hours later though, so i hit him back for 650. After that romo made a calculation error, so i could hit him again. Rambo hit Hook i think.

I now had only rambo in range, and rambo only me and romothecus. I was much bigger than rambo though, and my strat was more efficient than his. So, when we both started training op, i could do it much faster. I trained up enough to hit him all in. a day later i did that again. i was now 9.8K acres, and noone was left in my range. I stopped building about ~8500 build acres. If your interested in that build, you will see it with my final info.

I had noone left in range, so pumped offense bigtime. Towards the end of the round, the 71 goblin and spirit entered my range, and after that Ronne (Gave Bloody InsinQ hints). I hit them all, and ended up slightly over 12K acres  :)

something i dont think i mentioned anywhere: as a gobbo, you train offense before you train defense! Since wolfies are much more expensive, you can afford a lot less of them than you can buy shamans. So, you train wolfies first to keep your income big, then you train shamans.
