Roundwin Story

I won't make this hugely long and boring, but just huge and boring enough to make sure everyone leaves before the end.

I started dom again a few rounds back after a 20 round break or so and was shocked at the reduced playerbase, I thought that it'd probably be a lot easier now.

Was I ever wrong! this is a major compliment to most of the players who manage to get on top, the playerbase has become a lot smaller, but that's not made it easier, heck, it's probably made it HARDER.

It all started really weird, I thought I was playing with Petes, Emesel (interesting tricks you pulled eh!), slippery and a few others from the past round, but suddenly I was told everything kind of disintegrated and I was thinking of not playing.

I however bullied Gallagher and erix into teaming with me again, and I randomed two other packmates (rond and Mleduc) who I had never heard of, lastly, erix recruited one of his friends, Manno, of which he had expressely stated that he wasn't all that good anyway. We basically had a pack of relative nubs, so I really wasn't feeling our odds for anything even resembling this outcome.

This round started off fairly easy for me, most of the plans I had I excecuted fairly well and I had some nice hits (several within 10 offense of breaking), but 20 was just tearing up the place, ofcourse, they were deleted for the cheating gits they were!

From that point onward I started feeling good about my odds, and right around 3500 acres I was in pole position, with the nearest competitor about 400 acres behind. I chose to hit the no1 land at that time and thought I had the round locked down, then promptly the halfer's idiot realm mate posted a full sui on me and I got hit right after by aforementioned competitor. I thought stuff was pretty much over for me right there.

I however built a load of GT and pumped out some defspecs to be able to send fast again, and managed to latch on and at least stay competetive, but definetely behind the 45 dorf on military levels.

When 42 and 45 finally started fcking with eachother things turned positive for me again and I was back in the drivers seat, the last 1.5 weeks or so of the round felt like some sort of odd chess game, where everyone was watching eachother like a hawk, all training OP, and keeping eachother in, right down to day 49.

I had decided it was too risky to full send, since both rio (the river, 42) and the dorf had enough OP to threaten me rather directly, but having mutual war on 15, I decided to drop out of EG and bottomfood their top lyc, being able to leave 15K BB's made sure I couldn't be hit outside of a full sui.

I got home, sent full and prayed to dear god I wouldn't receive a last minute sui, ross, bless his little soul, disabled invasion before someone could, and here I am! FINALLY NO MORE FRIGGIN SECOND PLACE!

Thanks

Definitely one of the more interesting rounds, since it was an open race from start to finish, with no boring runaway sht.

I want to thank all my packmates, except Gallagher, who's rubbish!

Seriously, thanks to all my packies, and even some of the ghetto nubs who sometimes were willing to help on a wonder!

-Macros
