Halfling is a good-aligned race that can fast attack, explore, and convert in any ruleset. If you want to protect your platinum, Halfling is a prime choice due to their DP elite counting as a partial spy (1/3 when defending ops from other dominions, 1/5 when doing ops on others) - of course, this also means that you can lose DP when you fail spy operations on another dominion. In addition to protecting your platinum, it is very hard for other players to get spy ops on you, but you should not assume that this protect you from being underdefended.

Tech rounds

During the Tech Ruleset, Staff Masters lose their -40% casualties ability and increase in platinum cost in order to gain a new ability: +1 OP if you have the Spy Master tech. With this bonus, Halflings become much better attackers compared to the other rulesets, but it does require 3 techs. Because of this, many players choose to convert during Tech rounds, though it is also possible to attech to gain the bonus. Halfling explorer is unchanged during Tech rounds.

Imp rounds

In Imp rounds, Staff Masters' casualty reduction bonus is decreased from -40% to -30%, making them slightly less favorable attackers than in Base Ruleset.

General information

Pros

  • Spy race - platinum protection, easy to op others, nearly impossible to be sunk or stolen from
  • Great racial spell - gives 20% DP bonus (not cumulative with Ares' Call)
  • Low ore costs
  • Low food requirements

Cons

  • Inefficient
  • Master Thief is expensive
  • Loses DP on failed spy operations

Good for

  • Players with low activity
  • Thieves
  • Exploring for almost anyone
  • Somewhat experienced attackers/converters
