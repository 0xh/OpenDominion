General Information

This was the first round with 10-dominion realms. This round was very land- and NW-heavy with the first 10,000-acre attacker in seven rounds, five dominions over 8,000 acres and the highest top-10 requirement (7281 acres) since round 20. There were 19 dominions over 1,200,000 NW, the highest since round 41. Tjockogammal has now finished top-3 for three consecutive rounds and his sixth top-5 in the last nine rounds.

Roundwin story written by Sauce

Ivetza, JustaPeon and Tjockis didn't have enough information to write a detailed story so I'll contribute what I have. Just a little backstory, I was an techsploring sylvan in #53, MBalz Es-Hari. My packie vapro, Mustaf Herod Apyur Poupr, led early and so most of my information is with respect to him. I don't have details about how Ivetza earned the win, but I've got lots of stats and facts.

Day 3: Realm #59 is 12.3% bigger than 2nd place (5798->5122) their smallest guy is 500 acres, the next 10 realms are all within 300 acres. Looks like this round could be over already!

Day 4: The best OOPs were had by realm #53, vapro, Mustaf Herod Apyur Poupr, came out at 621 and grabbed 206 acres. Ladeda, Shaif Hirboush, grabbed 425 acres with 9 hits as a goblin. Other good OOPs were Mav3ricK, Great Balls of Fire (#20), who grabbed 295 acres over 8 hits as an orc and Boulder City (#66) a gnome, who scored 340 acres on 4 hits.

Peon in #21 has begun to convert. Ivetza becomes top op for the first time all round (90k) and has the best income for any attacker at 120k plat/hr. He was also the only top tier attacker to build DMs early and it's paying off now.

Day 19: We began to realize the threat that we were facing in Ivetza. He was the only top tier attacker who had a decent castle going and with CoG nobody stood a chance to keeping up with him. We warred them to take CoG which would hopefully slow him down enough. #62 also saw the threat and warred #20 for fireballing purposes. Day 21: We succeeded in taking the CoG from #20, #62 also took down Temple of Blessed from #0. Ivetza is recovering from being fireballed but he still has a large lead.

Day 23: Stanioso hits 4k in the same time that it took Quendi to do it last round, one of the fastest explorers to reach 4k. Ivetza also reached 4k. His op was comparable to xan and vapro but being 1 hit ahead means stronger economy. Sylvans like Robin Hood have begun converting lumber into plat and are rapidly ascending the ranks. The newest attacker to the top 10 is Macros, who at 42k pez has great income in addition to the ToB and his Military Genius tech. His op is still not high enough to be a threat yet.

As a result we see the top 10 filled with efficient sylvan explorers such as myself. #20 and #37 having begun 'sharing' the lair. For the remaining 23 days, the lair wouldn't go a day without being hit and changed hands every few days. The 2nd tier attackers could really only send when #37 controlled the lair. When #20 held it, everything stopped. Outside of the top 10, Sea you later #59 has been sitting around 12th and could be a threat later. Jeuno, who never recovered after his incident with xan, was all-in'd by Ivetza and wouldn't really grow past 4500 acres for the rest of the round.

Day 47: Ivetza finally sent, hitting the smallest dom in his range, an exploring sylvan, Suzy, Maid Marian (#92) for 372. It didn't take long for the 2nd tier to find a target. Mav was tripled by Peacefully Exploring (#21), Up and ATEM!! and Sea you later (#59) for 488, 391 and 366. With Mav's top 10 hopes gone, Ivetza could freely send every 12h for the rest of the round.

Day 48: Ivetza's next target was going to be BEKOBEH (#40) again, but Stanioso had wisely entered away mode to save himself. Ivetza hit inkus, pImp my Imps (#26) instead for 421.

Day 50: The last day scramble mixed up the top 10 pretty good. Perhaps the most influential move of the round came when Aisha, Positive Infinity (#11) had been stocking plat (85 million) for the final two weeks mass explored. She miscalculated the draftees needed and so instead released 20k Master Thieves so she could explore for 1500 acres. This left her with an insane 250k dp at over 6k acres. Within an hour, she had been 7-tapped for 2,451 acres. The first one to smash her was Tjockogammal, Up and ATEM! (#59) who grabbed enough land (432+216) to finish all the way in third place, shafting vapro into 4th. The second hit, by Poison Island (#61) was big enough (403+201) to put him into 8th overall. While most players were bf'ing for 100 acres, those lucky enough to still be home at 8am scored an extra 500-600 acres. The other newcomers to the top 10 were tetre, The Dark Paladin (#59) who fed off gobbos and kobbos while he sat outside of Ivetza's range; and myself who decided to stock plat outside of range and mass explore for 1300 acres while the other explorers trained massive dp.
