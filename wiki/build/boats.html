Boats

From The Dominion Encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

Boats are used to transport units back and forth when attacking. Not all units need boats as they fly or swim. In order to build (produce) boats, one must build docks. Every dock produces on average one boat every 20 hours, so 20 docks produce one boat per hour. Every boat can carry 30 units, except for Kobold boats which can carry 40 units. Docks are also used to protect boats from being sunk. Every dock protects 2.5 boats from sabotage.

Races that need boats

  • Dwarf
  • Firewalker
  • Gnome
  • Goblin
  • Halfling
  • Human
  • Icekin
  • Kobold
  • Lycanthrope
  • Nomad
  • Orc
  • Sylvan
  • The Nox
  • Troll

Races that may not need boats

  • Dark Elf - Spirit Warriors fly but Swordsmen and Adepts need boats if used.
  • Spirits and Undeads - the two elite units fly but the specs do not.

Races that do not need boats

  • Lizardfolk - all units swim.
  • Merfolk - all units swim.
