Almost Never Dies (or Immortality) can be a misleading concept in Dominion. No unit is truly immortal, but it is still a very good ability.

The units

There are four races with immortal units.

  • Dark Elf:
    • Spirit Warrior
  • Firewalker:
    • Phoenix
  • Spirit:
    • Phantom
    • Banshee
    • Ghost
    • Spectre
  • Undead:
    • Skeleton
    • Ghoul
    • Progeny
    • Vampire

Life & Death

Dark Elf

Spirit Warriors are only immortal when attacking targets that are at least 75% of the dominion's landsize. They die like normal units against wonders. Since they have no defensive power, they are not affected if you get hit. If your offensive power is <85% of your target's defensive powers, Spirit Warriors will be overpowered by the enemy and die like normal units. They will also die against realms with High Cleric's Tower.

Firewalker

Phoenix can only be killed when they are attacked by Icekins and are hence considered immortal.

Spirit and Undead

The only 2 races with all four units being immortal, except for the following situations:

  • When attacked by a Human/Nomad with Crusade. However, an attacking SPUD does not "die" when a defending HuNo has Crusade.
  • When attacked by someone from a realm that has the wonder High Cleric's Tower. HCT does not kill units on defense.
  • If your offensive power is less than 85% of your target's defensive powers, your units will die like any normal units.
  • Attacking wonders.

Raze

Immortal and near-immortal units (primarily Spirit Warriors) can be used to raze another dominion. This will decrease the target's defensive power (if their units aren't immortal). The purpose of a raze is to make it possible for someone else to attack the dominion afterwards. This is legitimate and not considered cheating if both the razer and the attacker are different people in the same realm. It may happen that someone first fails an attack (bounces) on someone and then another person from another realm hits the same dominion. This is only considered cheating if these two people of different realms coordinate their attacks.

Razes can however be done by anyone, but Dark Elves have nothing to lose if they are within 85% of the target's DP, since Spirit Warriors have no turtle anyway and thus cannot defend their land when they are at home.
