Realm

From The Dominion Encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The Realm is the group of people that you will be playing with during the round. There can be at most 12 people in the same realm, which in turn can consist of one or more packs and so called randomers (people that are not in a pack). The realm is your alliance and should work together to take wonders and wage wars, as well as help each other out with espionage and magic to find targets for the attackers or damage hostile dominions.

Council

Every realm comes with a Council that is used for discussing the game, strategies or just chat with each other.

Realm name

The monarch of a realm can set the realm's name, which will be displayed in the Realm View of the game as well as in Valhalla.
