We had taken the Obelisk from #39 prior to my hit and then #39 had taken the Lair from #28 when it was on cheap HP. #28 had not taken it straight back strangely but when #39 undeclared on them, they declared war straight back to take it 24 hours later. #39 then put their attention to the ToD from #33 but I managed to get a hit on devasas (40 dorf) in between them losing the Lair and taking the Temple:

As my OP would be much cheaper than his to replace, I wanted our pops to be as low as possible in order for our incomes to be tiny. That would give me the edge. With the gobbo still training wolf riders, I would be able to train OP at twice the speed going OP specs only myself. I also still had the ability to mod up on DP whereas he was fully modded already so with 10 days left, I was still in with a good shot.
