Goblin is often considered one of, if not THE fastest race in the game. The race is built entirely for speed. A strong population bonus of 20% and the necessity of using specs for DP. Goblins also have a 9-hour attacking unit in their elite OP allowing for even greater speed early on if attacking. A number of people enjoy playing Goblin as an explorer making use of the population bonus and spec DP in order to outgrow attackers early on. As with attacking, the key to succeeding as a Goblin explorer is to grow fast enough to be at the top early on before the great inefficiency of the race kicks in.

Plunder

Upon a successful attack, Hobgoblin may plunder (steal) a certain amount of the target's resources:

  • Gem and platinum plundering is limited to 20% of the target's gems and platinum.
  • Each surviving Hobgoblin can at most carry 50 platinum. Dead Hobgoblins are of no use to you.
  • You only plunder with Hobgoblins needed to win the attack. Sending extra will no longer let you plunder more.

Casualty Reduction

Shamans reduce casualties depending on what percentage of units at home they are. If 20,000 Shamans are at home with 6,000 other units, they will reduce casualties by: 

(20000/(20000+6000)) / 2 = 0.384615
38.46% less casualties
100 - 38.46 = 61.54% casualties

Tech rounds

Goblins are not changed much in Tech rounds.

Imp rounds

In the Imp Ruleset, Goblins have both advantages and disadvantages. The advantage is that due to their DP being spec-only, they are not affected by the increase in elite costs as much as all the other races. Their OP, however, is expensive so this does make attacking slightly harder. Additionally, they completely lose their gem and improvements bonus making investing into castle improvements infinitely harder.

General information

Pros

  • Use specs for DP making them the cheapest DP in the game
  • 9 hour on elite OP
  • Very fast
  • Good self spell
  • Very good attackers in Tech rounds

Cons

  • Expensive OP
  • Difficult for converting
  • Trolls have increased OP against goblins making goblins great targets late round for the more efficient troll

Good for

  • Intermediate to experienced players
