Rio's Top-Ten-Tips to finish with a flourish

1) Use any race at all

2) Have max smithies

3) Attack from oop (500-650 land depending on your simming skills)

4) Look to build somewhere between 150-250 schools

5) To get these schools, build 25 from each hit you make until you reach your goal

6) Other than OOP and the very last week, train only elites

7) Other than OOP and the last 2 weeks, don't build mods

8) Build 500+ DMs (mix homes and DMs 50:50 as you grow). You can supplement this by r/r once you pick up CC tech

8a) If you are a land-dependent race (such as WE or sylvan), replace DMs with LYs (if you're icer, you should still have room for DMs)

9) You'll be slow and in the noob-zone, so you'll need decent ratios. Get 200 spies, 100 wiz within the first 5 days and increase gradually. Spy:Wiz ratio of 2:1 usually suits me.

10) Be patient and hit safely. You're not Rio so don't take silly risks.
