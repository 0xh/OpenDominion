Orc is one of the newest races in Dominion. They are one of the few races that you need to attack with in order to do well. This is because their elite DP's strength is based upon prestige and the easiest way to gain prestige is through attacking other dominions. They can be played as a fast attacker (with a large spec number) or as a slow attacker (concentrating on elites). They do not work very well as a converter or explorer. There have also been occasions where people have obtained a high amount of prestige and then un-converted to being an explorer with the strengthened DP.

Tech rounds

In the so called Tech Ruleset, Orcs are considered one of the stronger races. This is due to their troops being unaffected by required techs and a created ability for Voodoo Magi. All casualties are reduced dependent upon the number of Voodoo Magi in their military. The formula is: 

(Voodoo Magis / Total Military) * 0.75 = Reduction in casualties on Offence and Defence.

One negative trait that Orcs have in the Tech Ruleset however is that they lose their 5% defensive racial bonus.

Imp rounds

In the Imp Ruleset, Orcs once again become stronger compared with other races. One of the big changes in Imp rounds is that elite unit costs are increased and Smithies are nerfed so having cheap elite units becomes beneficial. Orcs have cheap elite units and can make use of their spec units early making them a great choice for an attacker in this ruleset.

Pros

  • Cheap OP
  • Once Voodoo Magi have maxed out their DP due to prestige gained, their DP is extremely cheap

  • Can be played extremely fast
  • OP spell
  • In tech rounds one of the strongest races to attack with

Cons

  • Inefficient DP until high prestige is gained
  • Spell increases casualty numbers taken
  • Offensive elite's OP is reduced by target's GT percentage

Good for

  • Experienced attackers
