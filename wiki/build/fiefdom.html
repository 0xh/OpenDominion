Fiefdom

From The Dominion Encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

In Dominion Liege Lord there were fiefdoms which consisted of one Liege Lord and up to six Bannermen. Every hour, the bannerman received 3% of his liege lord's production of platinum, ore, lumber and mana, and the liege lord received 1% of all his bannermen's said productions. In reality, bonuses were only given out hours when the dominion either logged in or was updated. A liege lord would not receive bonuses from his bannermen's bannermen.
