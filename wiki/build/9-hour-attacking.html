9-hour attacking

From The Dominion Encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

9-hour attacking (9hr attacking or 9hring) is a special type of attacking unique to four races.

Races

  • Human/Nomad
  • Goblin
  • Sylvan

How it is done

The above races have elite attackers that return from battle after only 9 hours (and not 12 hours like all other races' units) and this should be utilized as much as possible since it allows you to attack twice while being on the same landsize. You begin by finding a target and send out on it. 9hrs later, your units are home again and you are still on the same landsize, meaning that you can attack once more on this landsize. It may be difficult to find a second target due to casualties but it is definitely doable early in the round.

Note that it is possible to 9-hour attack several times in a row. Three or four times in a row can sometimes be achieved (even without bottomfeeding) but one does then gain a lot of land while keeping one's army out which can be dangerous unless sufficient DP is trained throughout the entire spree.

Trolls and 11-hour attacking

Trolls can perform a similar strategy although it is rarely seen. It works only if the army consists of Smashers (possibly negligible amounts of Brutes as well). First, you send find and attack a target. Since Smashers are used for both offense and defense you will still have plenty of them at home. Seconds before your army comes home from the first attack, you attack once more. This means that for a few seconds, you will have left extremely low DP at home but time is on your side and once those seconds have passed, your first offensive battalion (or wave, if you will) returns home and the Smashers will provide with enough turtle DP to be safe (hopefully, since you have grown in landsize).
