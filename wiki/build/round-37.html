General Information

This was the first round to be won by exploring. The round is notorious for having an early, runaway Orc, against which an alliance was formed. Ross deleted the cheaters, which in turn gave DerangedBabysitsMe.mpeg and TetreExploreScript.css a great lead and they were never caught up with. This is still a controversial issue.

After this round, Ross implemented the explore bump, which is a certain landsize where the cost of exploration goes up. This was to nerf exploring in general.

Freedom (Blackreign's story)

Freedom

Last night, i was the biggest and strongest dominion by far. (Libertas, realm #43) If you asked anyone that day he would agree that this would be my third victory of dominion.

Because there were no targets inside my Elite Guard that i could outtrain, i was forced to leave its protective power and seek for targets in the Royal Guard. My realmies had been able to check out the most obvious threats and i decided that top sendable offense in my range was 300k. I sent out, hit an explorer, left 315k defense and had 15k incoming. Now, when i woke up, i saw my dominion at 4k acres, which was 2900 less acres than i left it behind with. Huge news, many things in our Town Crier. Apparently i underestimated things. While checking what happened, i noticed i got hit once more, had a lot less defense and 5 minutes later i was put back to the login screen after a click. I realised that i had lost 'too many' acres and was scripted by the anti-'farm' script. My account gone, vanished, erased from history. There won't even be a notice of my dominion in the lifetime rankings. My realmies are left baffled with my sudden absence and a red Town Crier. I had no way to retalliate, i did not even have access to all the information that could identify lame behaviour of my competitive colleagues in the game. Apparently, only my landsize was reason enough to attract a suicide and two razes which had an impact on my defense large enough to bring it down to a level on which multiple dominions were capable of hitting me. As it turns out, realm #59 has been misbehaving, in the sense that they employed a teamed effort to get me scripted. It envolves Bleach, a dark elf in realm #59 and realm #16 that did the hitting. Surely raze hitting is a frowned upon strategy, one that #59 has employed a few times this round. However, raze hitting for a different realm to invade and get someone scripted should be cause of intervention of a game administrator. For if that is allowed who needs Kamikazegames' Liege Lord Alliance game?

But even without the razes (three in total), i suppose my dominion would have suffered a similar fate. It's all in the game, and i knew what i was doing. I underestimated the forces, mods and suicide capabilities of a dominion that lied outside of my range.

Well, i guess i am finally free. Even now i'm thinking hourchange! as this game does require extreme activity. When i go to bed, i have to cast fools gold, but then i have to set my alarm because if i wake up a minute too late, half of my income will be gone from plat theft. And then during the day, one has to be there at least every few hours for the same reason.

Like many rounds, I started this one with a pack comprising of people i like chatting with - i grew tired of packing people solely for their dominion skills a long time ago. I valued the ability to talk to one another as human beings above being able to talk about dominion and plat/hr. Before the round, i got excited about a simple strategy that i 'discovered' on my strategy sheet. An orc with no bonebreakers, all savages for offense. Smithies, high barracks, and only voodoo magi as defense. In my eyes, the strategy was overpowered, even though Ross nerfed the Orc prestige bonus from 200% a while ago to 125% for this round. I got a packy excited about this, and the others took Lizardfolk en Dark Elf, which was nice for getting information on otherwise hard to check dominions. It was all routine for me, and i got frustrated a bit at times at some packies that they couldnt follow me or were less active. I was an ass at times really. Perhaps it was wrong to start playing. Sure, playing an overpowered strat and doing well is nice, but the game has cost me so much time and energy that could have been spent far better in people around me. Even so, the first few weeks were great. I enjoyed getting to the top together with my realmies and see my strat work out nicely.

For some reason many people judge others by their achieved landsizes in the past, as if the voice of one without much experience with the game is worth less. Reminds me of the l33t badminton people at the university that seem to think they have more right to play than others. This type of thinking dominated on the irc as it was back in the early rounds. "n00bs" were abused and scared away, people were mostly angry and talking crap all the time. Im glad that ever since the Dutch took over and started a new irc channel, new people were welcome and a different kind of ethical standard was held high. People have started the huggling sprees and perhaps respect eachother more.

I want to thank my packies - Scorpio, ActiveWaste, Lieke, Aisha and Jigsie - for their company, many great pieces of teamwork like getting the Imperial Armada (thanks Richie), scouting around for top ops (thanks Lori), helping eachother out when in need (thanks for all the cses Andreea), laughing together in the council and making this round interesting. Thanks Lieke and Jill for putting up with me and getting my head out of my ass. :)

For the Queen of Ice,

Robbie

-Blackreign
