Diplomacy

War and Peace are both pivotal parts of the game. They are both two of the most hotly debated subjects amongst players in the Dominion boards as many players feel that these declarations affect the game in an unfair way. They cannot be declared until the third day of the round.

War

War! Huh? What is it good for? Actually, a great many things when it comes to the game of Dominion.

Invasions

Opposing realms at war gain a +5% OP bonus against each other 24 hours after the war declaration is made. Once this 24 hour period is over, the bonus becomes "active". In addition to an OP bonus, realms also gain a +15% land gain bonus effective once the war is active. During a "Mutual War" where both realms declare war on eachother, the OP bonus is increased to +10% and land gain bonus is increased to +20%.

Black Ops

There are a great many restrictions within Dominion when it comes to casting spells, using espionage or gaining wonders. War opens all of these things up to the realms at war. Many of the most damaging spells and spy operations are only available at two times:

  • When invaded by someone in the past 24 hours
  • When at war with another realm.

By declaring War on another realm, these black op restrictions are lifted and are only restricted by land size difference between you and opposing dominions.

Currently, black ops are available immediately after declaring war and are an effective way of damaging a strong Dominion without the need of attacking them with OP. It usually takes a dedicated group of players within the realm to work together at the same time in order to mount a black op attack on a particualr dominion/group of dominions.

During Mutual War, black ops not only cause damage to your opponents, but will also give you small amounts of prestige dependent upon the damage done to the dominion and your wiz/spy ratio in comparison to their wiz/spy ratio.

Wonders

Taking a wonder from another realm requires a war declaration. You must wait for the war to become active, before you can attack their wonder. A wonder has Health Points and it takes this amount of HP in raw offence for your realm to destroy it and gain the wonder for yourselves. You do not all have to attack the wonder at the same time. You can take as much time as you want in order to chip away at the HP although other realms may declare war on the same realm and may "snatch" the wonder from you if you are not careful.

Clean war

A clean war is most commonly agreed upon when a realm declares war on another realm to take their wonder. The agreement normally means that no black ops will be used from either side and that the sole purpose of the war is to take the wonder. Whether invasions take place or not vary, but if they do not, it may be considered a NAP.

Peace

Peace was removed in round 64. Prior to that, Peace declarations, like War, took 24 hours to come into effect. The benefits of a Peace declaration on an opposing realm was as follows:

  • +5% DP bonus against opposing realm
  • No prestige lost when you are attacked by peaced realm
  • -10% land lost against peaced realm

The prestige and land loss changes were 2-way with both you and the peaced realm so if you invaded one of their dominions, you would have received less land and gained no prestige. The defensive bonus, however was only 1-way so they did not gain the defensive bonus against your realm. This was one of the biggest annoyances to a number of top players.
