Round

From The Dominion Encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Dominion is divided into rounds (abbreviated rd or rnd) which last for 50 days. Players cannot steal until day 4 or join guard until day 5.

Each round is assigned a ruleset but before the start of a round, Ross may announce changes to the upcoming round's ruleset. There is always a pause of a few days between the rounds.

The different stages

Rounds can be divided into different (more or less vaguely) defined stages. These stages fluctuate and the descriptions are generic guidelines.

Registration

Before every round, after changes have been announced and finalized, registration is opened. It usually lasts for three to four days. This period is sometimes called pre-round. You will not know your realm number but if you are in a pack, you can see their names if you log in.

Most players register at this point.

Day 1-3: Protection

The first 72hrs of a round (or the first 72hrs after you sign up) are called protection and are special in that your dominion will be under a magical state of protection, which means no dominion may take action against you and vice versa. This is when you get ready for the challenges ahead.

Day 4-16: Early round

This is when fast races dominate. Early converters convert at this point.

Day 17-33: Mid round

Those playing fast races will start to struggle now if they don't manage to maintain their speed. Medium-fast races get powerful. Around day 25, many converters convert.

Day 33-45: Late round

Fast races that didn't make it are dead now, serving as food to the stronger and more efficient races and to the fast and medium races that prevail. Some converters convert at this point, although converting mid round is much more common.

Day 46-50: Last few days

This is when you will see almost everyone having lots of mods and those who can do it without killing themselves in the process will pump specs. Many players cease to construct on acres gained at this point of the round as it simply isn't worth it anymore.

Day 50-xx: Unpaused/Post-round

Some rounds have continued beyond day 50, with nothing happening at this point entering the final rankings in Valhalla. There is no crazier part of the round than this. Suicides back and forth and complete mayhem.

Invasion and exploration disabling

At a supposedly random point during the last 24 hours of the round, invasion and exploration are disabled to avoid last minute suicides and mass exploration. It is random to the point that Ross manually disables the two sometime between early morning and noon game time. There have been a few rounds, since the implementation of this function, where invasion and exploration have been left open almost until the very end, leading to last-minute suiciding. Most recently, Round 46.
