General Information

Registration opened on the 5th of February, 2010.

Roundwin story

First off, I'd like to say that converting is so easy that it's hardly even funny. Yea, I did get very lucky that nobody ran and that there were plenty of suicides destroying the top so that I could break the surface more easily, but it was pretty ridiculous. I would have finished 4-5k at best this round if I had attacked with my activity levels.

Secondly, I'd like to thank my realm and pack. I could not have done it without them. Even if I would have rather not had Bond and Erik sui Lorian while they were still doing quite well, it at least accomplished its goal. Otherwise, thanks for all of the blackopping help from everyone and for everyone putting up with me being completely inactive :p

So, I originally wasn't going to play this round because I absolutely did not have the time to attack, however, between Rio's begging and protection running over the weekend, I decided that I would either explore or convert for the lulz and ops bitz or something. Now, I had always wanted to run an Icekin attacking OOP with all Frostmages while mass exploring at the same time, so this provided the perfect opportunity. I came OOP on 611 acres from wave 1 with something like 1800 frosties and 300 ice beasts by wave 6, making 2 hits in total at OOP and then 1 BF 12 hours later.

After those hits and mass exploring mountains I think I was ~1k acres by 36 hours out, which made me the largest and most efficient Icer explorer, having all frostie DP, good OMs, lower smithies than the other Icers, and 80 schools to finish the wiz tech after my OOP tech point gains.

At this point the weekend was over and I shifted into my work week inactivity of 2-3 logins per day. I pretty much just explored OMs, farms, and towers until I got to 800 OMs, then explored homes to max employment.

Sometime around 2300 I think, Dim got handed some acres and a wonder so we decided to snare and FB him as a police action as much as to keep him from running. Initially I had planned on converting at 3k, but pumping up to 10% towers at that point pushed my convert farther back to 3500.

I finished my convert with about 182k OP and 180k DP, and only 1 realm had even opped me during the entire process. This was top OP except for spooks in 14 who had just bounced on the 46 DE due to an ares recast. I hit a 100% gnome for some nice land and nice mountain which I built homes on and re-pumped my OP.

The rest of the round is kind of a blur between active weekends and inactive weekdays. Luckily for me, the major action took place on the weekends and I was able to do pretty well. I got suicided by the first gnome that I hit at some point, which kept others in the game, especially the 40 nomad. Eventually we sunk him and the round was pretty much mine.

Not much of a round story, but I wasn't really around for that much of it.
