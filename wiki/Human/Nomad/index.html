Human/Nomad are two races of Dominion. They are the same race and often considered the standard race in the game. The main difference is that Humans are good and Nomads are evil. They are suited for all types of players since their efficiency allows you to make quite a few mistakes and still manage a decent finish with them. They can be played as a fast attacker (with mod-buildings), as a slow attacker (with high DMs), as a converter or an explorer. It is a good race to learn to understand the basics of the game as it allows you to run many different strategies and buildings.

Tech rounds

In Tech Ruleset, Humans/Nomads are changed drastically. First of all Nomads are removed from the game entirely.

Furthermore, Humans receive a platinum production penalty which weakens them significantly as an exploring race. On the other hand, their Cavalry unit gets a +1 OP boost if you own both the Conqueror's Crafts and Tactical Battle techs. This forces you to use schools and convert rather than attack full-round if you want to play effectively. That way Humans are one of the most efficient races during the round but you lose a lot of speed, so it gets tough to fight for the top.

General information

Humans/Nomads are two of the four races capable of 9-hour attacking.

Pros

  • Efficient OP and DP
  • Cheap DP
  • 9hr return Elite OP
  • Diverse strategies
  • Quite forgiving when making mistakes
  • OP spell
  • In tech rounds one of the most efficient races

Cons

  • Expensive OP makes it hard to attack every 9 hours
  • In tech rounds platinum production reduction makes it harder to explore full round and, in combination with the tech requirement, pointless to attack

Good for

  • Exploring, attacking and converting: All players
