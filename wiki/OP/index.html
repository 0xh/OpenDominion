Offensive power

OP, short for Offensive Power, refers to how strong your army is on offense. All military units have a value for offensive power and Defensive Power (although it may be 0). In the scribes, under Military, you can see what values your race's units have.

Raw & Mod OP

Raw OP is the total Offensive Power of the troops that you're sending without including any bonuses that would affect it (such as prestige, Gryphon Nests or Forges).

Modified OP (also mod/modded OP, final OP) is your raw OP with all bonuses included.

Example

If you are a Merfolk and have to 2,000 Krakens and 1,000 Mermen, your raw OP will be: 

2000*4+1000*3 = 11000

Now, let's assume you have 500 prestige, 10% Forges and 5% Gryphon Nests and that it's Base Ruleset: 

(2000*4+1000*3)*(1+(500/250*2+10+5*1.75)/100) = 11000*1.2275 = 13502.5

Keep in mind that a race's units' values, as well as the multiplier for Gryphon Nests, may vary depending on ruleset and that rulesets are switched every round.

Bonuses

All bonuses to offensive power are additive, meaning that they stack and are not multiplicative. For example, 10% OP from a wonder, 5% from prestige and 10% from a tech adds up to a bonus of +25% OP. 

10 + 5 + 10 = 25
