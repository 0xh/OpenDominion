Sylvan belongs to the "good" races in Dominion. It is also one of the wizard races since their DP elite counts as half a wizard on defense and 1/5 wizard on offense. This makes them appealing to those players who enjoy blackopping others. When running high forests, their DP is very efficient and rather cheap. This combination makes them good explorers. The downside of this is that you are forced to run high Lumberyards for employment (and imps), since homes alone will not get you a sufficient forest percentage to make the DP worth it. The ideal amount for forest is said to be between 60-70%, depending on your playing style. Their main struggle, apart from getting the forest percentage up quickly while maintaining a good speed, is the fact that since they are landbased, they usually cannot afford to use high mods late-round. While most other races have the ability to increase their raw OP and DP late round by using GN/GT or temples, Sylvans have to keep around 60% of their land as forest at all times. Considering the need for other buildings such as Smithies, Towers, Ore Mines, Docks and usually some leftover Alchemies, the space for mod-buildings is limited.

Their OP is depended on the ruleset. In general, the Centaurs are 5 OP (5.5 with their spell) troop each with 9hr return for a decent price. But, in the tech ruleset you get a 25% casualty reduction. This makes it a lot easier to make back-to-back 9h hits so they have the potential to be really fast in tech rulesets.

Tech rounds

As already mentioned, Sylvans get a big boost in tech-rounds thanks to reduced casualties and 50% reduced rezoning costs. These features make them one of the best attackers in the game. Furthermore, the Dryad counts as a full wizard on defense if you own the Enchanted Lands tech. This might be appealing for explorers although the bonus is rather minor so we do not recommend teching with them.
